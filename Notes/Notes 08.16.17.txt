Positioning
    Static (defult) 
    Relative - to the original static position. It will first calculate where
    the element would have been, then moves it relative to that position. 

    Absolute - positioned relative to nearest positioned ancestor
        If the ancestor is not relative, child positioned absolutely 
        will be fixed to the browser.
    
    Fixed - Fixed to the viewport
